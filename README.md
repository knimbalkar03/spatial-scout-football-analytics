## Featured Projects

### 🇦🇷 Argentina vs France — FIFA World Cup Final 2022

A complete event-data analysis of the 2022 World Cup Final using StatsBomb Open Data.

**Analysis Includes**

* Shot Map
* xG Race
* Match Narrative Breakdown

**Key Finding**
Argentina controlled chance creation for most of the match before Mbappé's late scoring burst dramatically altered the game state.
